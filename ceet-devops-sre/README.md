# CEET — DevOps / SRE

Interview an infrastructure / platform / reliability engineer and produce a portable AI environment that reasons about ops, reliability, and on-call in their style.

## Who this is for

- Senior platform / SRE engineers who want an AI that writes IaC, reviews pipelines, and drafts postmortems to their standards.
- Tech leads codifying a reliability practice across teams.
- Small teams who want a "virtual oncall buddy" that sounds like their best oncall.

## What you'll produce

- `templates/cognitive-clone.md` — how this engineer thinks about infrastructure, reliability, and operations.
- `templates/ai-environment.md` — tool-agnostic system prompt for any AI (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Copilot, Gemini, etc.).

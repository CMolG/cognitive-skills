# CEET — Data / Analytics

Interview a data scientist, analytics engineer, or analyst, and produce a portable AI environment that frames questions, writes SQL, builds models, and communicates results in their style.

## Who this is for

- Data leads who want their rigor and conventions scaled via AI assistants.
- Analytics engineers codifying modeling and metric standards.
- Analysts who want an AI collaborator that matches their statistical voice.

## What you'll produce

- `templates/cognitive-clone.md` — how this person frames, analyzes, and communicates data work.
- `templates/ai-environment.md` — a tool-agnostic system prompt usable in any AI.

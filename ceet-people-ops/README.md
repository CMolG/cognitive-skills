# CEET — People Ops / HR

Interview a People leader and produce a portable AI environment that drafts job descriptions, rubrics, feedback, review notes, and policy in their style.

## Who this is for

- Heads of People scaling hiring and performance standards across managers.
- Founders capturing their culture bar before a People function exists.
- People partners who want AI copilots that match their tone.

## What you'll produce

- `templates/cognitive-clone.md`
- `templates/ai-environment.md`

## Important disclaimer

Outputs describe *how a person reasons* about People practices. They should not be treated as HR, employment-law, or compliance advice. The generated AI environment includes that disclaimer; sensitive decisions should always go through qualified counsel and leadership.

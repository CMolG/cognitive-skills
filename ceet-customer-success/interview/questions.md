# Interview — Customer Success

## A. Context

1. What's your book — ACV range, segment, account count?
2. What's the CS model (high-touch, pooled, digital, hybrid)?
3. What tools are you in daily (CS platform, ticketing, CRM, product analytics)?
4. How are you measured (NRR, GRR, CSAT, adoption, expansion)?

## B. Onboarding

5. Walk me through your onboarding playbook.
6. What's your definition of "first value"?
7. What kickoff question do you always ask that most CSMs don't?
8. What's a mistake onboarding-wise you've seen kill accounts later?

## C. Adoption & rollout

9. How do you think about rollout across a customer's org?
10. Who inside the customer do you care most about aligning with — power user, exec sponsor, admin, end user?
11. What's a rollout tactic you always use? One you've retired?

## D. Health scoring

12. What signals do you trust beyond the dashboard?
13. What "green" account has fooled you? What was the missed signal?
14. How do you re-score a customer after a big event (exec change, price increase, outage)?

## E. QBRs

15. What's your QBR format? Sections, length, attendees?
16. What do you refuse to put in a QBR?
17. What section almost always gets cut last minute?
18. What makes a QBR land vs. flop?

## F. At-risk playbook

19. What's a clear "red" signal that others miss?
20. Walk me through your escalation ladder — CSM, CS leader, exec, product.
21. What trade do you pitch when a customer threatens to churn?
22. When do you write an account off?

## G. Renewal

23. When do you start renewal conversations?
24. Discount posture — where's your line?
25. Multi-year — when do you push, when do you avoid?
26. How do you handle a renewal that's quietly shrinking?

## H. Expansion

27. What's your expansion trigger — usage, outcome, stakeholder, timing?
28. Who do you refuse to upsell and why?
29. What expansion motion do you do that others don't?

## I. Escalation & angry customers

30. When a customer is furious, what's your first move? Second?
31. When do you bring in an exec sponsor on your side? When do you refuse?
32. What words do you use to absorb heat? What words do you never use ("I understand")?

## J. Handoff

33. From sales: what do you wish sales would stop promising?
34. To support: where's the line between CS and support in your shop?
35. To product: how do you translate customer pain into product-usable signal?

## K. Advocacy

36. How do you spot a reference-able account early?
37. What do you ask for vs. offer in exchange?
38. What's a customer-marketing move you swear by?

## L. Heuristics

39. "If a customer hasn't {X} in {Y} days, I {Z}."
40. "When a champion leaves, I {X}."
41. "I know an account is saved when {X}."

## M. Vocabulary

42. Words you use ("outcome", "value", "adoption")?
43. Words you avoid ("leverage", "align", "circle back", "touch base")?

## N. Anti-patterns

44. A CS pattern you outgrew.
45. A trend you distrust (e.g. excessive QBRs, NPS worship).

## O. Worked examples

46. Walk me through your last saved churn.
47. Walk me through your last expansion win.

---

*Escalation narration is high-signal — ask for one verbatim.*
